ABOUT JEC

About JECJaya Engineering College (JEC) is an ISO 9001:2000 certified institution founded in the year 1995 with a focus on technological innovation, entrepreneurship and character building to the students. The college offers 9 UG courses and 7 PG courses. The courses like CSE, ECE and TEXTILE are accredited by NBA, New Delhi. JEC is Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The college management team consists of well-experienced and committed Academicians and Administrators.
